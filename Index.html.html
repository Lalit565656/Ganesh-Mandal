About Us

Established in 2005, Shree Ganesh Mandal Sindhari has been a central part of the community's religious and cultural life. Every year, we celebrate Ganesh Chaturthi with great enthusiasm, music, devotion, and unity.

Our mandal is dedicated to preserving and promoting our rich cultural heritage while serving the community through various social initiatives. We believe in fostering unity and spiritual growth among our members.

Our Mission

To create a spiritual hub that promotes devotion, cultural values, and community service while honoring Lord Ganesha.

Our Vision

To be a beacon of cultural preservation and spiritual growth for future generations.
